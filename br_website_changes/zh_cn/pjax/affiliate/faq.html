一般条款

Binary.com 联盟计划的内容是什么？

Binary.com 联盟计划让您向新客户推荐我们的网站并从中受惠。

If you’re a broker, we’d like to work with you. You will be introducing your clients to a unique and innovative product: the Binary.com trading platform. Your clients will love the Binary.com trading platform because we offer a complete binary options trading experience tailored to the needs of an exceptionally wide range of traders.

New traders can use our intuitive platform to learn about trading, practise trading, and gain trading experience. However, since new traders in the EU are considered as retail clients, they cannot trade binary options due to the current ban in place. Seasoned traders can use the Binary.com platform and take full advantage of the wide range of trading and analysis tools we have to offer.

我为什么要成为 Binary.com 联盟会员？

Binary.com 是拥有牌照及受监管的二元期权交易平台, 自2000年以来一直在运作。它有:

  • An international appeal with multilingual support in English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Chinese, Italian, Thai, Polish, and Russian;
  • An intuitive, web-based platform that’s instantly available to traders of all levels – anytime, anywhere; and
  • A competitive and flexible affiliate programme that can be adapted to your needs.

加入需要任何费用吗？

不收任何费用。加入我们的联盟计划是完全免费的, 将来也是如此。

“推荐客户”的定义是什么？

推介客户即通过您的专用联盟链接推介加入并已往他们的 Binary.com 账号注资的人。他们必须满足以下标准：

  • Becoming a Binary.com customer for the first time
  • Aged 18 years old and above (21 years old and above for clients in Estonia).

哪些人可能成为 Binary.com 平台的客户？

Anyone aged 18 years old and above (21 years old and above if residing in Estonia) and who is not the resident of a “restricted country” (as listed in our Terms & Conditions) can become a Binary.com client.

账户管理及追踪

我如何才能成为联盟会员？

方法很简单。只需进入注册页面并填写联盟注册表即可。我们会审核您的申请，并在申请批准后通知您。

I’ve forgotten my password. What should I do?

Don’t worry. You can simply reset your password.

如何更改付款方式？

登录您的 Binary.com 联盟账号并前往：财务→ 付款说明。

我收到联盟收益的方式和时间是？

您上个月的佣金将于每月15日前准时存入您的账户。

我怎么知道我赚了多少？

登录您的 Binary.com 联盟账号并前往：报告 → 详细活动报告

我可以生成哪些报告？

您可以生成各种包含深刻见解的报告，包括以下：

  • 点击和印象报表: 显示您的命中率和点击率
  • 国家报表: 显示您的点击率来源国家列表
  • 我的成员报告: 根据ID和注册日期显示您的客户列表

我是否可以让我推荐的客户不要直接注册，而是通过我的联盟专用链接注册，从而获得奖金？

Offering any kind of incentives to referrals residing in the European Union is strictly forbidden. Furthermore, we prohibit the use of unauthorised incentives, gifts, and payments to encourage customer signups. If we have reason to believe that you have engaged in such activities, we reserve the right to withhold any payments due to you. However, if you have a specific incentive in mind and your referrals do not reside in the EU, then please contact your account manager for further discussion and approval.
